Aries

March 21 - April 20

This Indica dominant hybrid was introduced by Sensi Seeds and named after Howard Marks, the Oxford graduate that became famous for smuggling marijuana in mid 1980's. Mr. Marks got arrested, did his time in federal prison and upon release published his autobiography - "Mr. Nice". This marijuana strain has dense buds that provide mellow mind-body melt. Best for evening and bed time use due to strong relaxation effect.TYPE OF HIGHInduces total body-mind melt, characterized by strong body relaxation, laziness and sleep.ORIGINA cross between G-13 and Sensi Seeds Hash plant.
